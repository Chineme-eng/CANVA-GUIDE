Module 1: Ideation & Workspace Setup

Strategy
The "All-In-One" Brainstorming Protocol
Don't start designing in Canva with a blank screen. Use Picao AI as your all-in-one platform to ideate your products, structure your content outlines, and generate listing copy in a single unified workspace before you ever open your design tool.
The "Sandbox" Folder Method
Keep your Canva workspace strictly organized to prevent shipping errors:

  • 📁 Work in Progress (WIP): Where you actively design.
  • 📁 Assets & Mockups: Where your listing images live.

Module 2: Licensing & Legality

Compliance
The Transformation Rule You cannot sell any Canva asset (free or pro) "as-is". You must combine elements, text, and colors to create a distinctly new composition.
Product Type Allowed? Condition
Finished PDF (eBook) Yes Must be flattened; assets not extractable.
Editable Templates Yes Must use the "Template Link" feature.
Standalone Clipart No Cannot sell raw graphics or transparent PNGs of Canva assets.
Trademarked Logos No Cannot trademark logos using Canva graphics (fonts/basic shapes are okay).

Module 4: Export & Delivery

Operations
The "Flattening" Protocol
When selling non-editable files (Workbooks, eBooks), select PDF Print and check the box that says Flatten PDF. This prevents users from copying the text or extracting your graphical elements.
The Hub PDF (Delivery Document)
Never send raw URLs to customers. Design a 1-page "Thank You" PDF in Canva that contains:
  • A warm greeting & brand logo.
  • A massive, unmissable button hyperlinked to your Canva Template.
  • A link to a 60-second YouTube unlisted video explaining how to use it.
  • Terms of Use (Personal vs. Commercial).

Module 5: Listing SEO

Marketing
Title Optimization Formula
[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keyword] | [Feature/Benefit] | [Format] Example: "ADHD Digital Planner | Goodnotes iPad Journal | Daily Schedule Tracker | PDF Download"
The 7-Image Listing Rule
Platforms like Etsy reward full galleries. Your mockups should include:
  • Image 1: The Hero (Clear, large text).
  • Image 2: Inside look (Multiple pages spread out).
  • Image 3: "How it works" (3 simple steps).
  • Image 4: Devices mockup (Phone, Tablet, Desktop).
  • Image 5: What's included (Bullet list).

Module 6: Pricing Psychology

Sales
The "Decoy" Bundle Strategy Price your single item at $9. Price your "Ultimate Bundle" (containing 5 items) at $17. The perceived value makes the bundle irresistible, instantly increasing your Average Order Value (AOV).
Price Anchoring
Always show the "total value" crossed out. (e.g., Total Value: $45 / Today: $12).
Charm Pricing
Avoid flat numbers. End prices in .99 or .97 for lower-ticket items ($4.99), or .00 for high-ticket premium masterclasses ($99.00) to signal luxury.

Module 7: The Final Launch Checklist

Execution

Run through this list before you push "Publish" on any platform.

  • Proofread all text within the product.
  • Verify the Canva Template Link actually works (open in Incognito mode).
  • Ensure "Flatten PDF" was checked for non-editable files.
  • Check color contrast for accessibility (WCAG).
  • Confirm the Delivery PDF links to the correct file.
  • Compress large PDF sizes below 20MB (use tools like ILovePDF if needed).
  • Add all 13 SEO Tags to your listing platform.
  • Set up your automated receipt email with the download instructions.
